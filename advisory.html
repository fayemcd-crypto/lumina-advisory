The transformation programme

From diagnostic
to transformation

Lumina Advisory works in three stages, each one building on the last. We work alongside your leadership team to illuminate the resistance and build the conditions for change.

01
Diagnose
2 — 4 weeks
We map the resistance: Across your culture and systems, the dynamics between your people, the judgement at the top, and the emotional truth your organisation is carrying. You get a clear picture of the source of resistance to change.
02
Design
4 — 8 weeks
Working with your leadership team, we design a programme built around what the diagnostic illuminated, across people, teams and systems. From leadership coaching and change champions to governance redesign, everything is tailored to where the resistance lives.
03
Transform & Change
3 — 12 months
We work alongside you through implementation, coaching leaders, embedding new behaviours, realigning systems and measuring the adoption that proves transformation has truly landed.
