The transformation programme

From diagnostic
to transformation

Lumina Advisory works in three stages — each one building on the last. We don't parachute in with answers. We work alongside your leadership team to illuminate the resistance and build the conditions for change to land.

01
Diagnose
2 — 4 weeks
We map the resistance across all four domains — the hidden architecture of your culture, the unspoken human dynamics, the distorted judgement at the top, and the emotional truth your people are carrying. You get a clear picture of what's actually stopping change from landing.
02
Design
4 — 8 weeks
Working with your leadership team, we design the interventions that will move you through the resistance. From governance redesign to leadership development to cultural alignment — everything is tailored to what your diagnostic revealed.
03
Transform
3 — 12 months
We work alongside you through implementation — coaching leaders, embedding new behaviours, realigning systems and measuring the adoption that proves transformation has truly landed. Not just on paper. In reality.
