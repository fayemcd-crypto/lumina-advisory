The Resistance Framework

Illuminating the Resistance

Lumina's framework maps the four domains where resistance lives. When you face the resistance, you can move through it. When these four domains align, transformation becomes inevitable.

01
Hidden Architecture & Systems
Where structural resistance hides
The world wasn't built for transformation, it protects stability. The governance structures, incentives and unwritten rules that shape how we work were never designed for change. Lumina illuminates the hidden architecture within our culture — and rebuilds the conditions that allow transformation to take root.
02
The Unspoken Human Dynamics
Where relationships shape transformation
Lumina shines a light on the human relationships needed for change to emerge — the unspoken dynamics between people, the trust that's missing, the conversations that never happen — creating the conditions where people can speak honestly, connect authentically and move together towards transformation.
03
The Distorted Lens
Where cognitive resistance operates
Under pressure, even capable leaders make poor decisions. Bias, overconfidence and cognitive shortcuts distort judgement at the moments that matter most. We develop the clarity and decision integrity to cut through the noise.
04
The Obscured Emotional Truth
Where human resistance shows up
Change is physiologically threatening. The nervous system responds to uncertainty the same way it responds to danger. We build the emotional maturity and self-awareness that allows leaders to respond rather than react.
